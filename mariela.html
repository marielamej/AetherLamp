Mariela's Process

Introduction

This section outlines the design and development process for the Aether Lamp from Mariela's perspective. The goal of this project was to create a minimalist and functional lamp that enhances the environment by simulating natural light patterns.

Research

In this phase, I focused on understanding the biological effects of light on our circadian rhythm. I explored user needs, lighting studies, and existing solutions in the market.

Design Iterations

The design of the lamp went through several iterations to ensure it was functional and aesthetically pleasing. Below are some early sketches and wireframes.

Technical Process

My work primarily focused on designing the electronics and ensuring the light control system was easy to implement. I utilized Arduino for the light control system, implementing various brightness and color temperature settings.

Final Outcome

The final Aether Lamp design integrates all the key features in a sleek and modern aesthetic. It is capable of simulating natural light patterns, enhancing user experience, and improving circadian rhythm.
